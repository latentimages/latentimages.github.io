Meet Tal Lazar

Recognized as one of the foremost film educators in the US, Tal has created and delivered workshops at prestigious institutions like Columbia University School of the Arts and the American Film Institute Conservatory. His insightful presentations are accessible online through major platforms such as Sundance Institute Collab and Berklee College of Music Online . Tal frequently conducts seminars around the world, with engagements at the Beijing Film Academy, University of New Orleans, Sacred Heart University, and Loyola Marymount University.

In addition to his educational contributions, Tal has enjoyed a successful international career as a cinematographer, his films available on streaming platforms like Netflix and Amazon. Looking ahead, he has several literary projects slated for release in 2025-2026. His articles can be found on Substack .
